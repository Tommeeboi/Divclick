As you can see, there is a grid of squares below this text. When you click play, one of the squares will turn black. This square will start moving around the grid, and your job is to click the black square! But there is a catch. If you click a gray square instead, you will lose a life! If you lose your last life... you die.

Exporting & Importing

T To export your current settings, click on the Export Settings button. This will copy some text to your clipboard. Create a text file and paste the text into it. Make sure to rename the text file so it ends in .divc (or .json).
To import those settings, click on the Import Settings button. This will bring up your file system.
